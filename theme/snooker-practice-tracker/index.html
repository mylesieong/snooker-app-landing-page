Snooker Practice Tracker

Snooker Practice Tracker provides a variety of drills (40+ drills in database) that helps you improve your cue ball control, break building and safety shots from basic to advanced.

Features

Drill database

• 40+ drills on break building, safety shot and snooker escaping shot
• Graphic and text explanation
• Exercises are categorized by difficulty and areas
• Beginner friendly drills available
• From basic to advanced

See drills

Practice Tracker and Metrics

• Practices tracking
• Performance metrics
• Cueing mastery estimation
• Compatible with different training methods

Exam mode

• A special mode that allows user to record at a shot-to-shot accuracy
• View pot success rate

Routine Builder (coming soon)

• User can design their own routines
• Choose drill type and quantity in routine
• Data collected from routine will be handled properly with other historical metrics

Explanation + Tips

Graphic illustration and explanation available for every drill. Video resource and tips (coming soon) can also be found in drill info page.

Day/ Night theme

• Day theme and night theme available in app.
• Switch themes for the best user experience
