Seated Exercise in Vehicles

It is a collection of exercises to do in seat during long ride or long flight.

If you ever had neck/back pain, foot numbness, blood circulation issue or DVT(Deep Vein Thrombosis) when traveling in a confined seat, you will find this app useful and applicable to most vehicles (airplane, bus, train, car, boat and etc.)
