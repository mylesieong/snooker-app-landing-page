Intelligent Collage Maker

Meet Intelligent Collage Maker, a free collage making app. It can find picture in user's mind faster and smarter with the help of machine learning technology.

• Organize your creativity by series and provide actions for pictures inside
• Preview series with auto-generated gif thumbnails

Output as collage/gif

• Create photo collages or gif with a series of pictures
• Many output options for you to choose

Search simliar pictures
